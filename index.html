Jackson Maher


Interested in creating work that explores the world around us? Me too.

An Emerson College grad, I like work that tickles my curiosity, and I'm curious about everything. For me, it's all about trying to understand this place we all live in. I've worked on projects ranging from critical analysis of television shows to online courses on big data to video recipes of new and different styles of food. If you can learn something new from it, I want to help make it.

Check out my portfolio or my web series Ideas At Play to see what I'm talking about, and reach out to tell me what you think about my work or if you'd like to work together.
